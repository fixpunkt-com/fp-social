# fp_social

TYPO3 extension to import and display social media posts from various platforms
directly in TYPO3 — manageable from the backend, without programming knowledge.

Posts are synchronized into your TYPO3 database and rendered from there. The
social networks are never contacted by your visitors' browsers, so no cookies
are set and no tracking takes place.

## Supported networks

* **Facebook** – posts from pages you administer
* **Instagram** – your own profile, or recent/top posts of a hashtag
* **LinkedIn** – posts from pages you administer
* **Wordpress** – all posts of a blog, or filtered by tag or author
* **YouTube** – latest videos of a channel
* **Bluesky** – posts from a profile

## Social Server

Access to the networks runs exclusively through a *Social Server*. By default
the hosted SaaS service is used — see [social.fixpunkt.com](https://social.fixpunkt.com).
If you do not want to use the paid service, you can run your own Social Server;
the required interface is described in the documentation.
